### Medical Center Booking System (Database Design)

**Objective:**
Developed a relational database schema to manage a medical center's operations, focusing on the relationship between doctors, patients, and appointments.

**Key Features:**
* **Patient Management:** Storage of personal data and medical history references.
* **Doctor Profiles:** Categorization by medical specialty and availability.
* **Appointment Scheduling:** A central table connecting patients and doctors, ensuring data integrity through Foreign Keys.

**Relational Logic:**
* Implemented **One-to-Many** relationships (One doctor can have many appointments).
* Designed the schema to prevent scheduling conflicts and ensure efficient data retrieval.

**Technologies:** SQL, [mySQL]
